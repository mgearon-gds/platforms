Digital Service Platforms

By creating an easy-to-use online service, you can help your audience get stuff done themselves - reducing confusion and saving time for your team.

Each of these platforms can be set up without technical knowledge. If you're familiar with APIs, you can connect them to your internal systems.

Put your forms online. No more sending out paper forms or copying information into your database - forms can connect directly to your system.

GOV.UK Forms is currently in testing - sign up to find out when it goes live.

Find out more about GOV.UK Forms

Collect and process payments. Add a simple link to GOV.UK Pay to send users to a trusted payment platform. You can also issue refunds and run reports.

  • Find out more about GOV.UK Pay

Send out multiple emails, text messages or letters at low to no cost. Automate your regular messaging, or set up templates you can reuse and edit.

  • Find out more about GOV.UK Notify

Make interactive prototypes - demo websites that look like pages on GOV.UK. You can use these to share ideas with your team or do user research.

  • Find out more about GOV.UK Prototype Kit
