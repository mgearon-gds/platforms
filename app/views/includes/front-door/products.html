Use GOV.UK Platforms to create your service

You can use one, some or all of the platforms for your online service.

With GOV.UK Platforms, you can:

  • build a form for your service
  • collect payment
  • send your users email, text messages or letters

GOV.UK Platforms are provided by the Government Digital Service. There is no procurement or need to manage multiple suppliers.
