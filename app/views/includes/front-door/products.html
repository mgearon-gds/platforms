Use GOV.UK Digital Service Platforms to create your service

Your users can complete a form, send you information, pay for things and receive notifications with GOV.UK Digital Service Platforms. They're low cost, easy to set up and have accessibility built in from the start.

You can use one, some, or all of the digital tools to support your service.
